# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone. We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, healthy community.

## Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving and accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers have the right to remove contributions that violate this code of conduct.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
